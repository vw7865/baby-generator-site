Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Baby Generator Pro. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Baby Generator Pro is designed with privacy in mind:

  • Photos: Photos you upload are processed locally on your device. We do not store, transmit, or have access to your photos.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we may collect basic information such as email address (if provided).
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our app's performance and features.
  • Subscription Information: Subscription purchases are handled through Apple's App Store, and we receive only transaction confirmations.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To process subscription transactions
  • To respond to customer support requests
  • To send important updates about the app (if you opt-in)

4. Data Storage and Security

All photo processing occurs locally on your device. We do not store your photos on our servers. Any data we do collect is encrypted and stored securely using industry-standard security measures.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and subscription management
  • Firebase: For authentication and database services (if applicable)
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management (if applicable)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through Apple's App Store settings

7. Children's Privacy

Baby Generator Pro is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
