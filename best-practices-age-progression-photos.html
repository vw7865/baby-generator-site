Best Practices for Age Progression Photos

Published: September 09, 2025

Creating realistic age progressions requires understanding how the AI processes images and what makes for the best input photos. Follow these professional tips for stunning results.

Starting with Quality Photos

The foundation of great age progression is a high-quality source photo. Use clear, well-lit images with the face clearly visible.

Understanding Age Stages

Baby Generator Pro creates progressions for baby, teen, adult, and elderly stages. Each stage requires different considerations for best results.

Facial Feature Clarity

Ensure all facial features are clearly visible in your source photo. The AI needs to see eyes, nose, mouth, and facial structure clearly.

Lighting Considerations

Even, natural lighting in your source photo produces the most realistic age progressions. Avoid harsh shadows or extreme lighting.

Multiple Progressions

Try creating progressions from different source photos to see variations. Each photo may produce slightly different but equally valid results.

Sharing Your Results

Age progressions make great conversation starters and keepsakes. Share them with family to see how traits might develop over time.
