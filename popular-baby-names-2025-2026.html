Most Popular Baby Names 2025-2026: Top Trends & Meanings

Published: July 08, 2025

Choosing a baby name is one of the most important decisions new parents make. Whether you're looking for something classic, trendy, or unique, here are the most popular baby names for 2025-2026 to inspire you.

Top Baby Girl Names 2025-2026

Olivia, Emma, and Sophia continue to dominate, but new trends are emerging. Names like Luna, Aurora, and Nova are rising in popularity, reflecting a love for celestial and nature-inspired names.

Top Baby Boy Names 2025-2026

Liam, Noah, and Oliver remain favorites, while names like Kai, Asher, and Ezra are gaining popularity. Modern parents are gravitating toward shorter, punchier names with strong meanings.

Trending Name Categories

Nature names (River, Sage, Willow), vintage names (Eleanor, Henry, Arthur), and gender-neutral names (Riley, Quinn, Avery) are all trending upward in 2025-2026.

Name Meanings and Origins

Understanding name meanings can help you choose a name that resonates. Many parents are choosing names based on their cultural significance or personal meaning.

Unique Baby Name Ideas

If you're looking for something less common, consider names from different cultures, nature-inspired names, or modern variations of classic names.

Tips for Choosing a Baby Name

Consider how the name sounds with your last name, think about potential nicknames, and imagine your child at different life stages with that name.
